The Merchant Baku is set within the magnificent, historical building that once housed the Caspian Shipping Company. Built in 1858, the iconic architecture reflects one of the most dynamic and important periods of Azerbaijani history. Located in the heart of the city, The Merchant Baku is an urban oasis where you can quench your thirst and satisfy your appetite.

Rooms & SUITES

The Merchant Baku has 63 luxurious, individually designed bedrooms inspired by a stunning array of French, Art Deco and Art Nouveau architectural styles. 16 rooms enjoy the benefits of a private balcony, while 9 have the convenience of connecting rooms making them ideal for families with young children. There is also one accessible room for anyone with special requirements. Please note, The Merchant Baku is a non smoking hotel.
